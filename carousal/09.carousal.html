Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.It was built between 1631 and 1648 and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.The land where the Taj Mahal stands was originally owned by the emperor.It is a popular tourist destination, known for its architectural beauty and historical significance.The Taj Mahal is made of white marble and is located on the banks of the Yamuna River.
